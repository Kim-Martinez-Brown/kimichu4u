My name is Kim Brown. I am a self taught crocheter. Thank you Youtube for the resources. I have been crocheting for a little over 2 years. I have not been able to crochet as much as I would like. However, I am hoping to change that with some life/school/work balance.

I follow patterns pretty well, however, I have not tried creating my own patterns. I lean towards amigurumi but am currently adventuring into cardigans. They just take FOREVER for me. Hence, the need for balance. In the future, I would love to get into Corner to Corner crocheting. With this style, I'm able to create nerdy blankets!
